Digital Pathology Review Portal

Concept demo for AI-assisted pathology review alongside Proscia Concentriq LS

This page is a mock frontend prototype showing how AI model predictions, slide metadata, quality control signals, and review notes could be connected to a digital pathology image management systemsurfaced in a web portal.

The goal is to demonstrate how our image management system could be organized for pathologists, researchers, or translational teams in a practical workflow.

Review Notes

    What this prototype is meant to show

    • A lightweight review layer on top of an image management workflow.
    • Case-level access to slide metadata, prediction results, and quality-control information.
    • Heatmap-guided review to help users inspect model-attended tissue regions.
    • A clear path from algorithm output to human review rather than a black-box prediction display.
